About

I am a self-taught artist.

I paint in all mediums including watercolour, acrylic and ink pen but I have always been particularly interested in oil paints.

I enjoy painting a wide range of subjects from portraits to city scenes and seascapes to equine.

Some of my favourite artists that inspire me are Ken Howard, Edward Seago, Degas, Sorolla and Tom Roberts.
